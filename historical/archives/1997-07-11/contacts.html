Who to contact at the XCF

If you would like to join the XCF, send your project submission to the Director @ the XCF (director@xcf.berkeley.edu)


If you are curious about the XCF in general you can send mail to the Active members @ the XCF (active@xcf.berkeley.edu)


If you have question or comments about the web pages send mail to me (billy@xcf.berkeley.edu)


Questions regarding specific projects should be directed to the members who are working on it.
