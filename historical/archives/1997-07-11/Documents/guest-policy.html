The XCF Guest Policy

Non-members may be given XCF accounts provided that:
  • Guests do not use XCF consoles frequently.
  • Guests do not heavily impact XCF or EECS resources.
  • Guests behave themselves.
  • Neither the faculty advisor nor the XCF membership objects.
The XCF Director has set this policy using the guidelines set by Professor Hilfinger's e-mail to a previous XCF Director, Jon Blow.
