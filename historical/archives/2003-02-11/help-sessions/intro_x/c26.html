Chapter 1. What is X Windows?

X Windows is a "network transparent, platform independent" windowing system. What does that mean? It means basically that you have a system which allows you to have various programs on your screen, some of which may not actually be running on your local machine. You can run programs on remote machines and have them show up or display on your local (or other) screen.
