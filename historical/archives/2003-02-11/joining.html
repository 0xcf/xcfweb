Applying is simple if you have a good idea of what you would like to contribute.

  1. Find out what we're all about by reading our statement of purpose.
  2. Read our project requirements page.
  3. Write a project proposal and email it to director@xcf.berkeley.edu for review.

If you don't have a project idea, you might want to check out the available projects page to get some ideas for a project of your own.
